in the attention economy, time is bartered for flickers of novelty. platforms design infinite scroll, alerts, and streaks to harvest focus, converting glances into revenue. algorithms model desire, amplify outrage, and reward impulsive taps, optimizing for engagement above meaning. creators chase metrics while audiences drift, auctioning their concentration to the highest bidder. attention becomes the scarce resource, shaping behavior and taste in a market where distraction is king.
